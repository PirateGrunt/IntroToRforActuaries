ChainLadder

Originally developed by Markus Gesmann in late 2007. Now developed by MG with Dan Murphy and Wayne Zhang

Implements many standard reserving techniques:

  • Multiplicative chain ladder
  • Munich chain ladder
  • Bootstrap
  • Dave Clark curve fitting
